"CABBAGE"

Cabbage is a leafy green, red, or white vegetable from the Brassica family, known for its dense-leaved heads. It's a popular ingredient in many dishes worldwide and is related to other cruciferous vegetables like Broccoli and Kale.

Key Characteristics:

  • Botanical Classification:Cabbage belongs to the species Brassica oleracea.
  • Types: Common types include green cabbage, red cabbage, and white cabbage.
  • Culinary Uses: Cabbage is eaten raw in salads (like coleslaw) or cooked in various dishes like sauerkraut and kimchi.
  • Nutritional Value: It's a good source of vitamins C and K, fiber, and antioxidants.
  • Health Benefits:Cabbage may help with heart and digestive health and may have cancer-fighting properties.
